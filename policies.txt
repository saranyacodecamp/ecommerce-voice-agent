RETURN POLICY:
Customers can return products within 30 days of delivery for most categories.
Luxury and designer products purchased on Tata CLiQ Luxury are eligible for return within 15 days.
Items must be unused, unworn, unwashed, and returned in original packaging with all tags, invoices, and accessories.
Electronics such as laptops, tablets, and smartphones must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
Innerwear, perfumes, beauty products, and customized items are not eligible for return.
Furniture and large appliances are eligible for return only if delivered in damaged or defective condition.
To initiate a return, log in to your Tata CLiQ account, go to My Orders, and raise a return request.
Reverse pickup will be arranged within 3-5 business days after the return request is approved.
Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the returned item passes quality inspection.

CANCELLATION POLICY:
Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped by visiting My Orders on the Tata CLiQ app or website.
Orders that have moved to the shipped status cannot be cancelled.
For Tata CLiQ Luxury orders, cancellation is allowed within 1 hour of placement only.
If you miss the cancellation window, you may refuse the delivery and it will be processed as a return.
Cancellation of orders placed during sale events may take up to 48 hours to process.

DELIVERY POLICY:
Standard delivery across India takes 4-7 business days depending on the delivery location.
Express delivery is available in select cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Pune.
Express delivery takes 1-2 business days and is available on eligible products only.
Free delivery is available on orders above 500 rupees.
Tata CLiQ Luxury orders are delivered with premium packaging and white glove delivery service.
Delivery to remote pin codes may take up to 10 business days.
A delivery OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number and must be shared with the delivery partner.
Delivery attempts will be made up to 3 times before the order is returned to the seller.

REFUND POLICY:
Refunds are credited back to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.
Credit card and debit card refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
UPI refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
Tata CLiQ wallet refunds are processed within 24 hours.
Net banking refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
Cash on delivery refunds are processed via NEFT bank transfer within 10-12 business days.
CLiQ Cash earned on orders will be reversed in case of returns.
Convenience fees and delivery charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective product.

EXCHANGE POLICY:
Exchanges are available within 15 days of delivery for clothing, footwear, and accessories.
Only size and color exchanges are permitted — product exchanges for a different model are not allowed.
The replacement item will be dispatched within 3-5 business days after the original item is received and inspected.
Electronics, appliances, and Tata CLiQ Luxury products are not eligible for exchange.
Exchange requests can be raised through My Orders on the Tata CLiQ app or website.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE PRODUCT POLICY:
Report damaged or defective products within 48 hours of delivery through the Tata CLiQ app or website.
Attach clear photographs of the damaged product, packaging, and invoice when raising the complaint.
A replacement will be dispatched within 5-7 business days after the complaint is verified.
If a replacement is not available, a full refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.
For large appliances, a technician visit will be arranged to assess the damage before replacement or refund.

PAYMENT POLICY:
Tata CLiQ accepts UPI, credit cards, debit cards, net banking, Tata Pay, CLiQ Cash, and cash on delivery.
Cash on delivery is available on orders below 10000 rupees in eligible pin codes.
EMI options are available on orders above 3000 rupees on select Tata cards and partner banks.
No cost EMI is available on Tata Neu HDFC Bank Credit Card on eligible products.
All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption and two factor authentication.
Tata Neu points can be redeemed on Tata CLiQ for additional discounts.

WARRANTY POLICY:
All electronics and appliances come with a minimum 1 year manufacturer warranty.
Tata CLiQ does not provide warranty directly — all warranty claims must be raised with the respective brand service center.
Warranty details including duration and terms are mentioned on the product page.
Physical damage, liquid damage, and damage due to misuse are not covered under warranty.
For premium brands on Tata CLiQ Luxury, extended warranty options are available at the time of purchase.

CLIQ CASH AND REWARDS POLICY:
CLiQ Cash is a reward currency earned on eligible purchases on Tata CLiQ.
CLiQ Cash can be redeemed on future purchases and is valid for 365 days from the date of credit.
CLiQ Cash is non-transferable and cannot be converted to real money.
CLiQ Cash will be reversed if the associated order is returned or cancelled.
Tata Neu points earned via NeuPass membership can be redeemed on Tata CLiQ at 1 point equals 1 rupee.

SELLER POLICY:
Tata CLiQ is a marketplace that hosts products from Tata group brands and authorized third party sellers.
All sellers on Tata CLiQ are verified and required to sell only genuine and authentic products.
In case of a counterfeit product, Tata CLiQ will take full responsibility and process a refund immediately.
Seller ratings and reviews are available on product pages to help customers make informed decisions.

LUXURY PRODUCT POLICY:
Tata CLiQ Luxury offers authenticated luxury products from brands such as Coach, Michael Kors, and Tommy Hilfiger.
All luxury products come with a certificate of authenticity.
Luxury products are eligible for return within 15 days only if unused and in original condition with all tags.
Luxury products are delivered in premium branded packaging with signature white glove delivery service.
Price match is not available on Tata CLiQ Luxury products.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY:
Tata CLiQ customer support is available 7 days a week from 8 AM to 10 PM IST.
Support can be reached via phone at 1800-266-0101, email at support@tatacliq.com, or live chat on the app and website.
All complaints are acknowledged within 24 hours.
Order and delivery related issues are resolved within 3 business days.
Payment and refund related issues are resolved within 7 business days.
Escalations can be raised by emailing nodalofficer@tatacliq.com if the issue is not resolved within the expected time.
